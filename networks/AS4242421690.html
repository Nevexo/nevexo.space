AS4242421690

A DN42 Network owned by WN-NETWORKS.

AS4242421690 is an extension of Project Interlink based on the DN42 decentralised network.

This network is new and currently doesn't have any active projects. When they're created they'll be listed below

and on the DN42/services wiki.


Project Features

Our AS is managed by multiple VyOS routers.

EBGP, IBGP & OSPF protocols are used at the edge & internal side of the network, RPKI will be implemented soon.

WireGuard is used for VPN tunnels to peers.
